Lab Design Methodologies of Digital Systems

In this lab you learn how to create an integrated circuit with state of the art tools. The lab is divided in three steps of increasing complexity.

Lab Basic Design Flow

This lab introduces the basic flow for the design of integrated circuits. The module that you are going to develop as an example is a simple 8-bit adder with registered inputs and outputs

Lab Intermediate Place and Route

In this lab, you are going to perform the place and route of a more complex system. More precisely, the goal is to develop for a small single-core RISC-V SoC called PULPino.

Lab Design Flow for Hierarchical Designs

In this Lab, you will create a Macroblock and use it in an NoC design. You will learn more advanced floorplanning and clock-tree-synthesis steps.

Lab Basic Test and Design for Test (DFT)

Basic design to understand the fundamentals of scan test and Design for Test (DFT).
